Today I’m happy to share that ggsci is now available for Rust. You can add the crates to your Rust project with Cargo:
cargo add ggsci cargo add ggsci-ratatui cargo add ggsci-ggsql ggsci for R started ten years ago as a small collection of color palettes inspired by scientific journals and science fiction, packaged as ggplot2 color ...
